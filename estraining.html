Electrical Safety Training

1. Electrical Safety Level-1

Duration : 3 hours
Batch Size : 30
Pre-Requisites : None
Target Audience : All

The objective of this workshop is to create awareness for personnel working at all levels; within a manufacturing facility. Topics covered in this course are:

• Basic Introduction and Terminologies
• Importance and elements of Electrical system
• Identification of Electrical Hazards
• Consequences of Ignorance
• Electrical Golden Rules

2.Electrical Safety Level-2 (Advanced)

This course covers all elements of Electrical Safety Level. In addition to the above, it further focuses on how to conduct an electrical inspection, safety talks etc. Topics covered in this course include:
• Conducting Electrical Internal Assessments
• Shop floor Assessments (Practical)

Duration : Level 1 + 3 hours
Batch Size : 30
Pre-Requisites : Access to the shop floor. Requires Electrical panel shutdown
Target Audience : Safety, Corporate and technical staff

3. Advanced Electrical Safety (Advanced)

Duration : 7 hours
Batch Size : 30
Pre-Requisites :
• Require details of two areas that client needs to be covered during training
• Shutdown of electrical equipment of two areas
• Multimeters and other electrical basic tools
Target Audience : Safety and maintenance team

This course covers an advanced level of electrical safety with technical content that involves:
• Conducting Electrical Internal Assessments
• Electrical safety of 2 specific areas of the premises (customised)
• Types of Safety Switch gears along with working principles
• Electrical PPEs
• Basics of Synchronisation Function
• Basic knowledge of IR (Insulation Resistance) Test and Thermography
• Shop floor Assessment (Practical)

4.Basic Earthing System (Basic)

This course is an introduction about earthing, it’s importance and consequences. Key elements covered include:
• Basic Introduction
• Importance and elements of an Earthing system
• Types of Earthing
• Consequences of poor Earthing system
• Equipment-specific requirement

Duration : 3 hours
Batch Size : 30
Pre-Requisites : None
Target Audience : All

Batch Size : 30

12. Lock out Tag out (Advanced)

Duration : 6 hours
Batch Size : 30
Pre-Requisites : Pre-requisite required as per annexure created for LOTO module
Target Audience : Safety, HOD’s, Department engineers, maintenance team, project team

This course covers all the elements of the Basic Lock Out Tag Out workshop, and additionally focuses on the development of LOTO procedure, LOTO form. This will be done for any one machine as requested by the Client. The course also includes practical application on that machine or equipment (with not more than 4 energy sources). Topics covered include:
• Development of LOTO procedure and LOTO form
• Identification of Locks and Tags for one machine (Customised)
• Practical session by applying LOTO procedure on the machine

13. Arc Flash Study + Surge Protection (Basic)

Duration : 3 hours
Batch Size : 30
Pre-Requisites : Electrical equipment details before the module
Target Audience : Safety, HOD’s, Department engineers, maintenance team, project team

This course covers the basic of arc flash, how it is generated and it’s relevant consequences. Topics covered include:
• Basic introduction and Terminologies
• Importance and elements of Arc Flash & Surge
• Difference between short circuit, arc flash and arc blast
• Consequences of occurrence
• Arc Flash study and it’s importance
• Why and where should Arc flash study be conducted
• Usage of Surge Protection Device (SPD) for different Electrical applications
