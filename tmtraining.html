Safety Sensitization

Duration : 4 hours
Batch Size : Maximum 25
Pre-Requisites : None
Recommended for : Top Management

The purpose of this course is to equip learners with the knowledge, skill and competence to recognize and maintain good health and safety practices in the surrounding environment. In this course participants are sensitized on:

• Understanding of Safety
• Need Vs. Minimum Compliance
• Proactive Vs. Reactive Safety
• Budgeting for Safety
• Effective Communication
• Safety Strategies
