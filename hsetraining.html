Health,Safety & Environment Training

1. HAZMAT

    This training enables participants to work safely while handling hazardous material. Key elements covered in this course are:
  • Hazardous Material classes
  • Prevention and Protection
  • SDS usage: Risk Assessment
  • NFPA Guidelines

Duration : 4 hours
Batch Size : 20-25
Pre-Requisites : None
Target Audience : Management and Supervisor

2. Behavior Based Safety

Target Audience: All
Pre-requisite: None

    This training includes:
  • Safety Awareness
  • Basics of Hazard
  • Basics of Risk
  • Safe traits
  • Roles and Responsibilities
  • Hierarchy of Control
  • Basics of PPE

3. Workplace safety (Office)

Duration : 6-8 hours
Batch Size : All
Pre-Requisites : None
Target Audience : Office Executive

    This training is meant for office executives to understand office hazards and their controls. The key elements of this training are:
  • Understanding Workplace Hazards
  • Ergonomics / Occupational Health Hazards
  • Managing Work-life balance
  • Stress management
  • Emergency Response

4.Construction Safety

Duration : 3-4 hours
Batch Size : 20-25
Pre-Requisites : None
Target Audience : All

    This course enables participants to understand the construction activity and their related hazards. The key elements of this training are:
  • Design Safety basics
  • Common hazards with general plant and equipment
  • Guidelines for hand and power tool safety.
  • Excavation and Digging
  • Lifting and Supporting
  • Scaffold safety
  • Fire prevention and protection
  • Indian Standards

5. Behavior Based Safety (Basic)

Duration : 4 hours
Batch Size : 15-20
Pre-Requisites : None
Target Audience : Top Management

This training is a process that creates a safety partnership between management and employees that continually focuses on people’s safety behavior. Key Elements of this training are:
• Safety Awareness
• Basics of Hazard
• Basics of Risk
• Leadership traits
• Leadership Strategies
• Change management
• Hierarchy of control
• Basics of PPE

6.Hazardous Work Safety (Advanced)

Duration : 4 hours
Batch Size : 15-20
Pre-Requisites : None
Target Audience : Top Management

This course cites safe processes for Hazardous activities. It includes:
• Work at Height
• Lifting and supporting
• Hot work
• Chemical Handling
• Energy Isolation
• Fire Safety
• Confined Space

7. Environment Aspect Impact Analysis

Duration : 8 hours
Batch Size : 20-25
Pre-Requisites : None
Target Audience : Management and Supervisors

    This training enables participants to understand the Environment Sustainability methods for industrial activities. The key elements of this training are:
  • Terminologies
  • Human Effect on Environment
  • Understanding Environmental Aspect/Impact
  • Analysis Methodology

8. Hazard Identification and Risk Assessment

Duration : 8 hours
Batch Size : 15-20
Pre-Requisites : None
Target Audience : Management and Supervisor

This training enables participants to identify hazards at workplace and their relevant controls. The key elements are:

• Terminologies
• Concept of Hazard and Risk
• Techniques of Risk Assessment
• Risk ratings
• Workshops

9.Hazardous Work Safety (Basic)

This course sensitizes participants on hazards they may be exposed to. It includes:
• Permit to Work
• Noise and Vibration
• Health and Hygiene
• Heat Stress

Duration : 6 hours
Batch Size : 20-25
Pre-Requisites : None
Target Audience : Management and Supervisors

10. Integrated Management System (Awareness and Internal Auditor- Certification Training)

The course creates an awareness encompassing the below standards:
• ISO 14001:2015 Environment Management System
• ISO 45001:2018 Health and Safety Management System

Duration : 2-3 days
Batch Size : 20-25
Pre-Requisites : None
Target Audience : All

11.Energy Management System (Awareness level)

This course is based on ISO 50001:2018- Energy Management System. This standard helps organization to identify significant energy use and their conservation.

Duration : 2 days
Batch Size : 20-25
Pre-Requisites : None
Target Audience : All
